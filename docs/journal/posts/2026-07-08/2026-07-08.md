# {{ page.meta.module }}: {{ page.meta.title }}

The crew investigate Blink's destruction, but initially don't find anything useful.
[Carnoc](carnoc-ashbrow.md) and [Ink](ink.md), and [Zeke](zeke-sinclair.md) are unable to locate Blink's logic core.
[Carnoc](carnoc-ashbrow.md) tries to view [Murderbot](murderbot-v3.md)'s memories, but can't make any sense of them.
The crew delivers the economic model to [Ingus](ingus.md) at [Canyon Heavy.Market](canyon-heavy-market.md) in exchange for their reward.
At [Dex](dex-miro.md)'s request, [Babushka](babushka.md) scans [Murderbot](murderbot-v3.md)'s memories but she's blocked from investigating deeper.
[Babushka](babushka.md) examines Blink's body and discovers that his white pseudo milk is actually blood.
A deeper scan of [Ink](ink.md) reveals that Blink strangled [Ink](ink.md) in his cryo pod, swapped their blood colors, and took his place.
The crew has [Ingus](ingus.md) hack into Blink's memory core, uncovering a directive to eliminate the Minotaur.
[Ingus](ingus.md) reveals he wants to crash all of **The Company**'s markets.
[Yandy](yandy.md) summons the crew to ask about the artifacts.
On the way to the docks, the crew witnesses a station resident violently coughing and undergoing on ominous transformation.
