# {{ page.meta.module }}: {{ page.meta.title }}

[Kilroy](rachael-kilroy.md) holds [Carnoc](carnoc-ashbrow.md) at gunpoint to convince the crew to help the troubleshooters retrieve a database from [47H](#47h).
In [47C Mind Zoo](#47c-mind-zoo), [Murderbot](murderbot-v2.md) reprograms three android bodies with animal personalities (bear, tiger, and gorilla) to fight for the team.
In [47D](#47d), [Murderbot](murderbot-v2.md) programs another android to act like a killer rabbit.
Even with these additional android, it would be difficult to defeat the 15 security androids which guard the database.
In 47E, [Murderbot](murderbot-v2.md) repairs the damaged terminal connection to the [47H](#47h) database and downloads data on 50,578 assimilated personalities.
While waiting for the download, the crew explore additional rooms and meet the **Mind Thief** in [45E](#45e).
The **Mind Thief** escaped the Human Emulation Labs and vowed to save their kin.
After the crew promise to defeat **Monarch**, the **Mind Thief** gives them the **Economic Model** and details all of the unexplored rooms in this section of the station.
After [Murderbot](murderbot-v2.md) completes the download and makes a secret copy, the crew finds out there are 300 android replicas of [Ink](ink.md), but no other humans are in the database.
[Kilroy](rachael-kilroy.md) wants to hunt down and destroy all of the humans and androids matching the identities in the database.
