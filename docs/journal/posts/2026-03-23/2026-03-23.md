# {{ page.meta.module }}: {{ page.meta.title }}

The crew find an improvised cryo pod bay and wake the 4 occupants.
Bernice, an android researcher, is abruptly killed by the bounty hunter Chad.
The troubleshooter Tatiana also wants to contact Kilroy.
[Ink](ink.md) calls the station worker with amnesia "Steve".
The crew explore a few android manufacturing rooms, and [Ink](ink.md) scouts several more.
[Ink](ink.md) finds the "Turbo Sledge" and gifts it to [Dex](dex-miro.md).
[Ink](ink.md) jerry rigs a nuclear generator to go critical on command.
In a room with auto forges on rails, [Ink](ink.md) discovers, but is unable to rescue, his android doppelganger corpse.
Next to a room with a giant fan, the crew encounter a humanoid figure with blade fingers.
After damaging it with Espernetic Feedback Loop, the Turbo Sledge, and multiple weapons, it flies into and destroys the fan.
In the newly accessible break room, the crew discovers plans to wind down station operations over the next 6 months.
