# {{ page.meta.module }}: {{ page.meta.title }}

The chosen retreat behind a locked door, which the fallen androids are slaughtered by automated machine guns.
[Ink](ink.md) miraculously avoids injury from a reckless test of changing gravity in an unusual corridor.
Hank follows and killed on impact, but [Ink](ink.md) quickly cleans up and hides the body.
Only [Dex](dex-miro.md) suspects Hank is dead.
A floating woman mutters about her lost toolbox and then attacks the crew.
After exploring a few more rooms, the crew find the toolbox which calms **Helen**.
[Zeke](zeke-sinclair.md) learns **Helen** wants to fix the station, but returning the toolbox causes her to disappear.
Inspecting the toolbox reveals many useful items, which are distributed amongst the crew.
